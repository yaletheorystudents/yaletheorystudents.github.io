Yale Theory Student Seminar

This is a place for theory-minded people to gather for a weekly lunch seminar. This semester (Spring '25), we (mostly) meet on Thursdays at 11:45 am (for lunch) and the talk starts at noon. The presentations are on papers, results, conjectures, or anything theory-oriented. In order to keep things more casual and interactive, presentations are on the board. We meet at the FDS common room (i.e. room 1307) in Kline Tower.

If you are interested in joining the mailing list, please reach out to {xifan.yu,felix.zhou}@yale.edu.
