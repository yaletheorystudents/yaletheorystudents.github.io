yale theory student seminar

This is a place for thoery-minded students and postdocs to gather for a weekly lunch seminar. We will meet on Wednesdays at 12 (for lunch) and the talk will start at 12:15. The presentations will be on papers, results, conjectures, or anything theory-oriented. In order to keep things more casual and interactive, presentations will be on the board. We meet at the YINS common room, on the 3rd floor of 17 Hillhouse. If you are interested in joining the mailing list, please reach out to Marco Pirazzini (marco.pirazzini@yale.edu) or Siddharth Mitra (siddharth.mitra@yale.edu).
